VARIETIES

Sofa's

A sofa is a long, upholstered seat typically designed to accommodate multiple people, offering comfort and style in living rooms, lounges, and other seating areas.

Cushioned Chair

Cushioned Chair are designed with extra padding and cushions on the seat, backrest, and often the arms for enhanced comfort.

Office Chairs

An office chair is a type of chair specifically designed for use in office settings, featuring ergonomic features like adjustable height and lumbar support to promote comfort and productivity during long hours of work.

Wingback Chairs

Wingback chairs exude timeless elegance. Their high, curved backs provide excellent support and a touch of grandeur and a classic, elegant look.

Tub chairs

Tub chairs are characterized by their curved back and arms, resembling the shape of a bathtub, offering a cozy and inviting seating solution.

Cafe chairs

Cafe chairs are typically lightweight, stackable, and feature simple designs, making them ideal for cafes, restaurants, and outdoor dining areas.
